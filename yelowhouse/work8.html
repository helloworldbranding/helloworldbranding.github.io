NestAway HQ
HSR Layout, Bangalore

For another co-living property of Helloworld, we went for warm colours like yellow and orange to exemplify happiness, excitement and enthusiasm.

While choosing light fixtures, we went for triangular luminaires to go with the funky theme. Instead of following a singular color scheme, a color and design harmony was ensued as is visible in the fluid graffiti and playful textures on the flooring, all to keep it as vibrant as we could.
