Birsa Munda Badminton Court
Chaibasa, Jharkhand

One of our most special projects was amongst the green lanes of the small town of Chaibasa in Jharkhand. Witnessing the drive and the ‘never give up’ attitude of young minds of Chaibasa, we took up the task of face-lifting a dingy, uninviting and insipid library into a welcoming, accommodative and alluring one under the patronage of the District Development Commissioner of Chaibasa.

The library has been kept very formal in its outlook with a touch of an elegant colour like mustard for the beams and columns, not taking away from the solemn feel of a library. Ivory on the walls makes the space bright, open and inviting just like an ideal library should be. All the woodwork has been done in deep tones of brown for a more cohesive look.
