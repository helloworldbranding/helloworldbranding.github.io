Birsa Munda Badminton Court
Chaibasa, Jharkhand

“Success is where preparation and opportunity meet.” This opportunity and a space for the right kind of preparation are being provided by the District Administration of Chaibasa to its young and determined players. We collaborated with the District Development Commissioner on another project which involved revamping the district Badminton Court.

The wide galleries of the court were contrasted with a serene, tranquil colour of Blue. Blue was our instant choice as it is known to instigate confidence, inspiration, imagination and freedom. Overall the tones of white and blue make for a holistic look. In order for the façade to look coherent, tones of white, light blue and royal blue are used. While light blue signifies serenity and calmness, dark blue signifies virility, strength and depth.

The posters of eminent badminton players with their brief biographies were put up on the walls to make the players aware of the struggles, challenges and dedication that the sport entails. The poster of Birsa Munda on the façade was put up for the players to draw inspiration from the epitome of strength, valour and determination while also keeping the tribal spirit alive.
