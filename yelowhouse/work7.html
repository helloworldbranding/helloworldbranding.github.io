NestAway HQ
HSR Layout, Bangalore

NestAway office revamp was exciting projects of ours which involved transforming a dreary and sully space into a vibrant, vivacious and vivid one.

Brief

Current office is too crowded. Looks old, faded and cluttered. Need to add life to it.


Approch

Maximum makeover with minimal expenditure is our main mantra. Basic things need to be refurbished and a lot of paints to add freshness and newness to the space.
