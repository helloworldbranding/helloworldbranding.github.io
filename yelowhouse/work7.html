NestAway HQ
HSR Layout, Bangalore

NestAway office revamp was exciting projects of ours which involved transforming a dreary and sully space into a vibrant, vivacious and vivid one.

The Cafeteria which was open with washed out colours and an open washing area was made to look nice, cozy and fuzzy with warm lighting

The plastic roof was replaced with a terracotta gable which gives a homely vibe while adding to the warmth. A huge conference room which was mostly lying unused due to leakage issues was converted into 6 meeting room set using glass, grass carpet and wooden flooring.

In contrast to the cafeteria, the conference rooms have a more formal and business feel to them flaunting complimenting tones of green and wood texture. It also incorporates maximum space utilization through partitions and dividers.

The Graffiti and green plants complete the look by adding that last pop of colour! As this was a budget restrained project, we ensured to reuse old furniture, glass and plywood to keep the whole project less hard on the pocket.
